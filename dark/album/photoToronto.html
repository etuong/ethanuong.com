Toronto, Canada

Toronto is the most populous city in Canada and the provincial capital of Ontario. The city is very diversed (with over 80 different ethnic communities) and half of the city's residents were born outside Canada. There are six districts with Old Toronto housing the most tourist attractions. Some awesome things to do include walking along the famous Yonge Street (Eaton Mall), eating a peameal bacon sandwich at St. Lawrence Market, and admiring the city's beautiful glass architecture! If you have time, make sure to check out the Niagara Falls!
