Vienna, Austria

Vienna is the capital and largest city in Austria. It also serves as the country's cultural, economic, and political center. Vienna is also said to be "The City of Dreams" because it was home to the world's first psycho-analyst - Sigmund Freud. Known for its musical legacy, it played an essential role as a leading European Music Centre, from the great age of Viennese Classicism to the modern era.
